About me

Hello and welcome to my personal site, which will likely change as I continue to tinker around with it.

A little bit about me - I was born in Tokyo and spent my early childhood between there and Shanghai before moving to NYC with my family. Since graduating from Wellesley College, I've worked for tech startups in Boston, San Francisco, and now, back in NYC. I love the fast paced hustle of startups and thrive in building some structure around the chaos. I've worked in operations and product roles at early to mid staged startups (Series A and B level funding), mainly focusing on helping them optimize their processes as well as scale efficiently and cost effectively.

During my previous roles, I saw the value in having technical knowledge and realized how it could make people work more effectively and have a stronger impact in their organization. As such, I've decided to take some time to learn how to code and feel empowered to build out some of my own ideas. In the meantime, I am also consulting for operations and consumer experience projects - drop me a line!
