About me

Hello and welcome to my personal website, which may change its form as I continue to tinker around with it.

A little bit about me - I was born in Tokyo and spent my early childhood between there and Shanghai before moving to NYC with my family. After graduating from Wellesley College, I've worked in the tech startup scene in Boston, San Francisco, and now, back in NY. I love the fast paced hustle of startups and thrive in building some order in the chaos. I've worked with early to mid staged startups (Series A and B level funding), scaling them and optimizing efficiency and costs in operations and product roles.

Over my last couple of roles, I realized that people with technical knowledge were able to work more effectively and have a stronger impact in their organization. As such, I've decided to take some time to learn how to code and feel empowered to build out my own ideas. I am also consulting for operations and consumer experience projects - drop me a line!
